# Conversions + CRG + Finance

GambChamp tracks every monetisation event from broker postbacks and turns them into structured payout accounting for both brokers and affiliates.

## Conversion ingest

Broker postbacks carry a `status` field that maps to one of three conversion kinds:

| Kind | Idempotent? | Trigger |
|------|-------------|---------|
| `REGISTRATION` | Yes — once per lead | Lead submits personal data on the broker platform |
| `FTD` | Yes — once per lead | Lead makes their first deposit |
| `REDEPOSIT` | No — can repeat | Lead makes any subsequent deposit |

`REGISTRATION` and `FTD` are written at most once per lead. Duplicate postbacks for these kinds are silently discarded. `REDEPOSIT` creates a new `Conversion` row each time, allowing the platform to track lifetime deposit volume.

The postback handler emits conversions via `src/server/finance/emit-conversion.ts`. Raw broker status strings such as `"REDEPOSIT"` and `"REGISTRATION"` are mapped to `ConversionKind` before the `Conversion` row is inserted.

## Payout rules

Payout rules define how money flows between the platform, brokers, and affiliates.

**Broker payout rules** (`BrokerPayoutRule`) determine the **revenue** GambChamp earns from a broker per conversion. **Affiliate payout rules** (`AffiliatePayoutRule`) determine the **payout** GambChamp owes an affiliate. Margin = revenue − payout.

Rules are resolved at the time of reporting. The most-recently-created matching rule wins (`resolveRuleAt`). Affiliate rules can be scoped to a specific broker, or set globally and applied when no broker-scoped rule exists.

The four rule kinds are described in [Concepts: payout kinds](/docs/conversions-crg/concepts#payout-kinds).

## CRG weekly cohorts

**CRG (Conversion Rate Guarantee)** is a contractual commitment from a broker to achieve a minimum FTD rate. GambChamp tracks this weekly:

- Cohort window: **Monday 00:00 UTC → next Monday 00:00 UTC**.
- Each `CRGCohort` row accumulates `cohortSize` (leads pushed) and `ftdCount` (FTDs received) over the week.
- Settlement runs **30 days after `cohortEnd`** to allow postback delivery lag. The `crg-cohort-settle` cron job processes all eligible cohorts nightly.
- If the broker's actual FTD rate falls below their guaranteed rate, a shortfall is calculated and recorded on the cohort. See [Concepts: CRG shortfall](/docs/conversions-crg/concepts#crg-shortfall).

## Invoicing

Invoices are generated monthly. Two invoice types exist:

- **`BrokerInvoice`** — what GambChamp invoices the broker (revenue).
- **`AffiliateInvoice`** — what GambChamp pays the affiliate (payout).

When all conversions in a billing period originate from exactly one broker, the `AffiliateInvoice` is linked 1:1 to the `BrokerInvoice` via `AffiliateInvoice.brokerInvoiceId`. Multi-broker periods do not carry this link.

CRG shortfall amounts settled within a period are appended as `crg-cohort-<id>` line items on the broker invoice.

All monetary amounts are in **USD only** (v1).

**PDF export** returns a JSON placeholder in v1. Real PDF rendering is planned for v2.

## P&L dashboard

The P&L summary page at `/dashboard/finance/pnl` aggregates revenue, payout, and margin across a configurable date range with filters for affiliate, broker, and GEO.
