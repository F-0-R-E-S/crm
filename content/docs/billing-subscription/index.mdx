# Billing + Subscription

GambChamp charges tenants a platform subscription fee. This is **separate** from the internal broker and affiliate invoices that track payout accounting — see [Concepts: platform vs internal invoices](/docs/billing-subscription/concepts#platform-vs-internal-invoices).

## Plans

Four tiers are available:

| Plan | Price | Leads / month | Max brokers |
|------|-------|--------------|-------------|
| Trial | Free (14 days) | 100 | Unlimited |
| Starter | $399 / mo | 5,000 | 10 |
| Growth | $599 / mo | 25,000 | 50 |
| Pro | $899 / mo | 100,000 | Unlimited |

Trial subscriptions are created automatically when a tenant is provisioned (via seed or onboarding signup). The 14-day clock starts from `Subscription.trialEnd`.

## Stripe Checkout and Portal

Upgrading from Trial or changing plans launches a **Stripe Checkout** session. The user is redirected to Stripe's hosted payment page, completes payment, and is returned to `STRIPE_BILLING_RETURN_URL`.

Plan downgrades, payment method changes, and invoice history are managed through the **Stripe Billing Portal**. Tenant admins access both flows from **Dashboard → Settings → Billing**.

Subscription state is kept in sync via Stripe webhooks — GambChamp processes `customer.subscription.{created,updated,deleted}` and `invoice.{paid,payment_failed}` events at `POST /api/stripe/webhook`.

## Quota enforcement

On each lead intake request, `enforceQuota(tenantId, delta)` compares the current-month lead count (from `LeadDailyRoll`) against the plan limit. If the limit is reached or exceeded, the intake route returns:

```
HTTP 429  plan_quota_exceeded
```

Existing lead rows are not modified. Only **new intake** is blocked until the next billing period begins.
