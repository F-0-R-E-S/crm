# How to build an analytics preset and share it

This guide walks you through configuring the analytics dashboard for a specific view (e.g. "Weekly US ops"), saving it as a named preset, and sharing it with a stakeholder who doesn't have CRM access.

## 1. Open the analytics dashboard

Navigate to `/dashboard/analytics` (keyboard shortcut `Y` from anywhere in the dashboard).

## 2. Configure the filter bar

Use the filter bar at the top of the page to narrow the data to the view you care about:

| Filter | What it controls |
|--------|----------------|
| **Date range** | The primary time window (last 7d / last 30d / custom) |
| **Affiliate** | Limit to leads from one or more affiliates |
| **Broker** | Limit to leads routed to a specific broker |
| **GEO** | ISO-3166-1 alpha-2 country code (e.g. `US`, `DE`) |
| **Group by** | How the X axis is bucketed (day / hour / week) |

## 3. Enable the compare toggle (optional)

If you want period-over-period deltas on the chart:

1. Click the **Compare** toggle in the filter bar.
2. Choose a comparison mode:
   - **Previous period** — same-length window immediately before your selected range.
   - **Year ago** — same calendar range 12 months prior.
   - **Custom** — pick any date range manually.

The chart will render two overlapping line sets: solid (current) and dashed (comparison).

## 4. Save the preset

Once the filter bar reflects the view you want:

1. Click **Save preset** (top-right of the filter bar).
2. Enter a name — e.g. `Weekly US ops`.
3. Click **Save**.

The preset is now available in the preset dropdown and persists across sessions.

## 5. Star as default

To have this preset load automatically whenever you open the analytics page:

1. Open the preset dropdown and find your preset.
2. Click the **star icon** next to its name.

The star makes this preset the default for your user. Only one preset can be starred at a time — starring a new one de-stars the previous default.

## 6. Share with a stakeholder

To send a read-only view to someone without CRM access:

1. With your preset active, click **Share**.
2. The share dialog shows a TTL selector (default 30 days) and a preview of the URL.
3. Adjust the TTL if needed, then click **Copy link**.
4. Send the URL to your stakeholder.

The share link at `/share/analytics/:token` renders the dashboard read-only — no filter controls, no preset management. When the TTL expires the page shows an "expired" banner instead of the data.

<Callout type="info">
Share links capture the filter-bar snapshot at the moment of creation. The underlying data is live — the stakeholder sees fresh numbers each time they load the URL.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
Share links do not require authentication. Treat them like a signed URL — do not share them in public channels if the data is sensitive.
</Callout>
